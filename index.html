About Me

Li Chien is a full stack web developer, electronica composer, nature-watcher, Philadelphia Eagles fan, and professional Overwatch player wannabe, or in other words, a human being. He is skilled in front and back end web development and specializes in CSS stylings. Li enjoys working on projects in a team to create spectacular user-friendly products and thinking outside of the box to solve complex problems. Li received his Bachelor of Arts in Music Composition from Denison University. His compositions are quite diverse, as he has worked with Washington D.C. rapper Darren Hanible and New York City string quartet ETHEL.
